Pita Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • Palm oil
  • Fresh farm eggs
  • Salt
  • McCormick Italian mix herbs
  • Black pepper

Steps

  1. Break open some eggs and transfer to an appropriately sized sauce bowl
  2. Heat up a frying pan.. like really hot!
  3. Put some oil and let it smoke!
  4. Layer the eggs onto the pan
  5. Immediately drizzle summadat salt
  6. Baste them beauties
  7. The undersides should start to brown. Add some spice and black pepper
  8. Serve on a toast!
