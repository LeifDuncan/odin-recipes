Pita Bread Recipe

Ingredients

  • All purpose flour
  • (Optional: wheat flour)
  • Salt
  • Yeast
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Prepare your dough by mixing the flour, salt and yeast. Add a bit of wheat flour if that's your thing.
  2. Let it set for 2 minutes. If no bubbles form, your yeast is dead.
  3. Heat up a pan with olive oil
  4. Take bits of dough and shape into discs
  5. Cook it!
